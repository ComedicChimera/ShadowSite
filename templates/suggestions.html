What Is a Suggestion?

A suggestion is a description of an addition, removal, or altercation to Whirlwind or any of its related components that would improve said component. For example, here you could suggest a language feature or perhaps an improvement to the standard library or compiler. You could even suggest an improvement to our documentation or the look of the website.

Please note, that if you make a suggestion, and it is accepted, your name will be added to the README.md on the Whirlwind Github repository as well as several other things outlined in the "Next Steps" section of this page. All that being said, making a suggestion does not grant you the title of a "Whirlwind Core Developer" nor does it necessarily give you a right to the actual suggestion implementation or feature. What you put down here is merely a suggestion and not an actual description of the nuance of any given feature.

Making a Suggestion

When making a suggestion, keep a few things in mind. First and foremost, you do not need to describe your idea in excessive detail. Simply give a clear enough description of your idea such that it is well understood. Please note that while we deeply appreciate you taking the time to contribute to Whirlwind, there is a probability your suggestion will either be declined or not reviewed at all. We do try to avoid the latter, but it is possible for it to occur. If either of the above happens to your suggestion, please do not take it as an indictment of you or your idea. It may simply have been that your idea would take too much time to implement or is not in line with our vision for Whirlwind. Or simply that we did not have time to fully review your suggestion. All that being said, if your suggestion is approved, we will contact you via the email you will provide to let you know that your suggestion has been approved. Thank you again for your contribution.

Next Steps...

If you suggestion is accepted, you will contacted to alert you that your suggestion has been approved. Additionally, you will be given an opportunity to submit a pull request to the appropriate repository implementing your suggestion with a guarantee that it will be reviewed. If your request is accepted, you may even be given an opportunity to push code directly to the corresponding repository. Please note that this may happen, if we deem your code contribution of Whirlwind quality. Additionally, your permission may be revoked if it is abused and/or in violation of our code of conduct with regard to contributions.
